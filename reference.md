1. 神经符号与算术逻辑 (The "Math Brain")
Trask et al. (2018). Neural Arithmetic Logic Units (NALU). (奠基作，解决数值外推问题)
Madsen & Johansen (2020). Neural Arithmetic Units (NAU). (改进了 NALU 的不稳定性，推荐使用)
Sahoo et al. (2023). Learning Equations with Latent Variables. (如何从神经表征中提取解析式)
2. 因果强化学习与不变性 (The "Causal Soul")
Schölkopf et al. (2021). Towards Causal Representation Learning. (阐述了为什么要解耦因果机制才能泛化)
Arjovsky et al. (2019). Invariant Risk Minimization (IRM). (解决 OOD 的核心算法，通过寻找跨环境的不变量)
Elias Bareinboim (2023). Causal Reinforcement Learning. (目前最权威的因果 RL 综述)
3. 动力学发现与符号回归 (The "Equation Finder")
Brunton et al. (2016). Discovering governing equations from data (SINDy). (如何从噪声中提取 F=ma 级别的方程)
Vajdi et al. (2024). SINDy-RL: Interpretable and Efficient Model-Based RL. (将 SINDy 引入 RL 的最新前沿)
Udrescu & Tegmark (2020). AI Feynman: A physics-inspired method for symbolic regression. (物理学家马斯克好友写的符号回归经典)
4. 贝叶斯自适应与 OOD 泛化 (The "Adaptive Core")
Zeng et al. (2025). BAP-SRL: Bayesian Adaptive Priority Safe RL. (即你提到的 BAPR 基础)
Ghosh et al. (2021). Why Generalization in RL is Difficult: Epistemic POMDPs. (解释了为什么 OOD 是认知黑洞)
Kirsch et al. (2023). General-purpose meta-learning of functional inductive biases. (如何学习函数归纳偏置)
